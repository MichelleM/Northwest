About COR

The Winnipeg Construction Association’s Board of Directors and the CSAM Advisory Committee guide our organization. We’re funded entirely by a % levy (3-4%) on top of an employers Workers Compensation Board of Manitoba premium. We are proud to run a sleek, efficient organization at as little cost to you as possible.

Although we are not a government organization, we have a close working relationship with the WCB and the Workplace Safety and Health Branch (WSH) to promote safe work practices and procedures throughout the entire construction industry. We are also a participating member of the Canadian Federation of Construction Safety Associations and have authority to grant COR™ Certification (national safety-program accreditation) within Manitoba.

Our objectives are to:

  • Provide information regarding accident prevention methods and changes to health and safety regulations
  • Develop resources, tools and training programs to enable contractors to meet their legislated responsibilities
  • Guide the industry to establish comprehensive safety programs tailored to individual company needs
  • Act as authority with jurisdiction to grant COR™ Certification & Small Employer COR™ Certification Essentially, we provide advice in the form of training, education, information and resource material to align with our slogan: Practical Solutions for a Safer Workplace
